MarketX – Multi-Vendor Marketplace (Admin Portal)

MarketX is a multi-vendor marketplace platform where multiple sellers can register, list products, and manage sales while administrators monitor and control the entire system through a centralized dashboard.

The Admin Portal allows administrators to manage vendors, products, orders, and customers while tracking marketplace performance through real-time analytics.

Key Features

Dashboard Overview: Displays total revenue, vendors, products, orders, and customers.

Role-Based Access: Different dashboards for Admin, Vendor, and Customer.
Vendor Dashboard

The Vendor Dashboard allows sellers to manage their products, track sales, and monitor performance within the marketplace.

Key Features

Revenue Overview: Displays total revenue generated by the vendor.

Product Management: View and manage listed products.

Order Tracking: Monitor pending and completed orders.

Trust Score: Performance rating based on sales and customer feedback.

Analytics: Monthly revenue chart and insights into top-performing products.

This dashboard helps vendors efficiently manage their business and track marketplace performance.

Customer Portal

The Customer Portal allows users to browse products from multiple vendors, make purchases, and track their orders within the marketplace.

Key Features

Product Browsing: Search and explore products across different categories.

AI Recommendations: Personalized product suggestions based on user activity.

Shopping Cart: Add and manage products before checkout.

Order Tracking: Track order status and delivery updates.

Vendor Chat & Compare: Communicate with vendors and compare products before purchasing.
